Our Café

At Café 11, we believe that great games deserve great refreshments. Our menu features freshly brewed coffees, energising smoothies, and delicious snacks that keep you fueled before and after play. The warm lighting and comfortable seating create the perfect ambiance for friends, families and teams to unwind together.

Whether you're celebrating a win or simply catching up, our café offers a variety of beverages and bites to suit every taste. We also cater for small parties and corporate events—contact us to curate a custom menu for your next gathering.
